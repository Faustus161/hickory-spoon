Our Story

From the clubhouse kitchen to the best table in town.

What started as a small restaurant inside Santiam Golf Club has grown into something much bigger — a full-service restaurant, a serious bar, and a destination for the whole community in the Willamette Valley.

We're open to everyone — golfers, families, date nights, and anyone who wants good food with a view. Comfort-driven plates made with care, a bar stocked for the post-round crowd and happy hour regulars alike, and event spaces that host everything from birthday dinners to company gatherings of 200.

Families always welcome. Groups of 6 or more are encouraged to call ahead — we'll take care of you.
